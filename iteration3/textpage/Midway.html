The battle of Midway is a famous battle of the U.S. Navy.
The battle of Midway Island started on June 4, 1942. In this battle, the US Navy successfully repulsed the Japanese Navy's attack on the atoll of Midway Island, and the Japanese army was defeated in the naval battle.
The victory of the campaign deeply inspired the morale of the anti fascist allies and won the victory of reversing the situation of the Pacific War.
