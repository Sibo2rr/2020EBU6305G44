Taierzhuang victory, also known as Taierzhuang campaign, the battle of Lunan or bloody Taierzhuang. There are several versions of the beginning and ending time of the Taierzhuang campaign. It is generally believed that it began on March 16 and ended on April 15, 1938. The battle was composed of Tengxian battle, Linyi battle, Taierzhuang battle, Japanese retreat and Chinese pursuit.
In the fierce battle that lasted for one month, about 290000 Chinese troops took part in the war, and about 50000 Japanese troops took part in the war. More than 50000 Chinese casualties and more than 20000 Japanese casualties were reported.
It dealt a blow to the arrogance of the Japanese aggressors and strengthened the confidence of the army and the people of the whole country in adhering to the war of resistance.
This campaign inspired the morale of the whole nation, changed the international audio-visual situation, eliminated the prestige of the Japanese aggressors, and annihilated a large number of Japanese forces.
This great victory is another victory for the Chinese people since the all-round Anti Japanese War, following the Great Wall campaign and Pingxingguan great victory. It is the biggest victory since the Anti Japanese War, and also a major victory for the National Revolutionary Army in the battle of Xuzhou.
