The sneak attack on Pearl Harbor is a military incident planned by the Japanese government during World War II to sneak attack on Pearl Harbor, the Pacific Naval Fleet base of the United States, which became the fuse of the Pacific War in World War II.
In the early morning of December 7, 1941, the aircraft carrier and mini submarine of the Japanese Navy suddenly attacked Pearl Harbor, the Pacific Fleet base of the US Navy in Hawaii, and the airports of the US Army and Navy on Oahu Island.
The Pacific War broke out.
